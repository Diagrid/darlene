Nevada, 1872

Your journey West is on the brink of failure - the crops aren’t growing, you have no money, and you’re running out of time; you won’t survive another winter without a change in fortune.

Your wife, Darlene, is all you have left. She has gone out in search of water, but she hasn’t been back for some time.

Sitting upon your porch, you hear a rumble, and dust rises in the distance. She’s in danger.

You have to find Darlene, and quickly, before the desert consumes her.
